Hook loop

  1. Trigger - the actuator of behavior
  2. Action - the behavior done in expectation of a reward
  3. Variable reward - getting something new, nice emotions/skills/knowledge, getting problems solved
  4. Investment - user puts something into a product

What it is done for?

  • For increasing the value of a company by driving higher customer lifetime value
  • For higher pricing flexibility
  • Social product sharing

Technical engagement elements:

  1. Progress bars
  2. Achivement system
  3. Accessibility
  4. Colored provokable buttons
  5. Notifications
  6. Emails

Conslusions:

  • Use habit-forming strategy only for good things
  • Be careful with things you spend yourself on, otherwise you'll be HOOKED!
